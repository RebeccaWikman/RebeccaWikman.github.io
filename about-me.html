About Me/Contact

I'm a game designer living in Stockholm, Sweden. My preferred focus is narrative design. 

I enjoy RPGs, time management, and simulation games, and occasionally play a MOBA or two with my family. Though my main interest is narrative design, I also enjoy finding the connections between narrative and gameplay, the conceptual and the environmental. I find that this is a good way to create a seamless, immersive experience for many players, including myself.

I enjoy reading and creative writing in my free time, as well as a host of crafts like crochet, perlers, sewing, and baking.
