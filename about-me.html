About Me

I'm a game design student at Future Games, set to graduate in the spring of 2021. 

I enjoy RPGs, time management, and simulation games, and occasionally play a MOBA or two with my family. The presence of a story and lore is an important factor for me and I love creating worlds in which players can be immersed and have fun. 

I enjoy reading and creative writing in my free time, as well as a host of crafts like crochet and perlers. Though my main interest is narrative design, I also enjoy the challenge of combining this with gameplay concepts and environmental storytelling to create a seamless, immersive experience. 

At the moment, I'm experimenting with how simple a game can be and yet still be engaging. See more on this in my "Ongoing Projects" section. 

Have a question or a project you'd like me to weigh in on? Feel free to contact me!
